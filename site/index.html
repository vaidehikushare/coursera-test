Chicken

Start with fresh boneless skinless chicken thighs. Boneless skinless chicken breasts would also work with this recipe! Combine all the seasonings in a small bowl and sprinkle on both sides of the chicken. You can cook the chicken on a grill pan, over a traditional grill or even in your oven! This chicken is ready in about 15 to 20 minutes!

Sushi

Place your desired ingredients onto the rice. I’ve got salmon, avocado, and cream cheese.Roll it up tightly, using a bamboo mat. Then use a sharp knife to cut the sushi roll into pieces. Now you’re ready to eat the rolls with some soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi, as desired!

Beef

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook, 5 minutes per side. Remove from the skillet and set aside. Add onion garlic to the skillet; cook until onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Stir until all of the browned bits have mixed in with the liquid. Pour in the beef stock, cover and simmer over low heat until very tender, about 2 hours.
